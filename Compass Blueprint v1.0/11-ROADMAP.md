# Roadmap

> *"The roadmap describes how Compass evolves from a clinical knowledge platform into an intelligent clinical reasoning system."*

---

# Purpose

The Roadmap defines the long-term evolution of Compass.

It is not a feature checklist.

It is a progression of capabilities.

Each phase builds upon the philosophy, principles, domain model, and clinical journey established in the Blueprint.

Implementation may change.

The direction should remain stable.

---

# Guiding Principles

Every phase should:

- Strengthen the Clinical Journey.
- Improve clinical understanding.
- Preserve evidence.
- Maintain trust.
- Respect the Domain Invariants.

New technology should support these goals rather than redefine them.

# Phase 1 — Foundation

## Goal

Build the core clinical platform.

### Capabilities

- Patient Management
- Clinical Journey
- Clinical Events
- Evidence Management
- Care Team
- Medication History
- Diagnosis Tracking

### Success Criteria

Compass can accurately represent a patient's clinical journey.

---

# Phase 2 — Knowledge Platform

## Goal

Transform data into structured knowledge.

### Capabilities

- Relationship Graph
- Timeline Navigation
- Advanced Search
- Cross-referenced Evidence
- Clinical Summaries
- Version History
- Traceability

### Success Criteria

Users can understand the complete clinical story rather than isolated documents.

---

# Phase 3 — Clinical Intelligence

## Goal

Assist human understanding.

### Capabilities

- AI-generated summaries
- Referral letter generation
- Chronological reasoning
- Medication history analysis
- Trend identification
- Case comparison
- Explainable recommendations

### Success Criteria

AI helps users understand the patient's journey while preserving evidence and transparency.

---

# Phase 4 — Decision Support

## Goal

Support clinical reasoning.

### Capabilities

- Risk assessment
- Differential diagnosis assistance
- Treatment response analysis
- Guideline awareness
- Clinical reminders
- Knowledge recommendations

### Success Criteria

Compass becomes a trusted assistant while keeping humans responsible for decisions.

---

# Phase 5 — Knowledge Network

## Goal

Learn across many patients while protecting privacy.

### Capabilities

- Anonymous population insights
- Pattern discovery
- Outcome analysis
- Research datasets
- Benchmarking
- Federated learning

### Success Criteria

Knowledge gained from one case can improve understanding of future cases without compromising patient privacy.

---

# Phase 6 — Learning System

## Goal

Continuously improve clinical understanding.

### Capabilities

- Continuous knowledge refinement
- Learning from outcomes
- Adaptive clinical models
- Evidence evolution
- Personalized assistance

### Success Criteria

Compass becomes an evolving clinical knowledge system grounded in evidence.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Compass should become a platform where:

- Clinical knowledge is preserved.
- Evidence remains trustworthy.
- AI is explainable.
- Clinical reasoning is transparent.
- Caregivers and clinicians collaborate effectively.
- Every patient benefits from accumulated knowledge.

---

# What Success Looks Like

Success is not measured by:

- Number of users.
- Number of AI models.
- Number of features.

Success is measured by:

- Better clinical understanding.
- Better communication.
- Better continuity of care.
- Better patient outcomes.
- Greater trust.

---

# Roadmap Principles

The roadmap is directional.

Individual technologies may change.

Implementation strategies may evolve.

The philosophy, principles, and domain truths should remain stable.

---

# Founder Notes

## Why does this document exist?

To provide a long-term direction for Compass while ensuring that growth remains aligned with its philosophy and domain model.

## What future decisions should it guide?

Product planning, prioritization, architecture, AI capabilities, research, and long-term investment.

## What must never change?

Every phase of Compass should strengthen clinical understanding, preserve evidence, and improve the patient's journey.
